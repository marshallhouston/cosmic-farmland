shipping a pr used to mean 6 context switches. now it's one command.

i built /ship after i caught myself refreshing a pr tab waiting for CI. classic bike-shed work. valuable-feeling, zero value.

the skill watches checks, investigates failures instead of punting them back to me, squash-merges, deletes the branch, removes the worktree, pulls main. then shuts up.

the principle: a failing check is a signal, not a dead-end. if it failed, look. if it's flaky, re-run once. if it's cosmetic on a non-required check, merge through.

lives in cosmic-farmland on github. take the ideas and run.
